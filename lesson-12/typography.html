Typography

The font family used on this website is Roboto, medium, 500. It's a Google Font and it has been used on Android devices and Google services such as Google Maps, Youtube, Google Play etc. According to the Google Fonts website, Roboto has "dual nature" because it is mechanical and geometric but it's also friendly and has an open curve.

While building the website, different font weight and font decoration can be used. Futhermore, the font size will be changed according with the viewport (small, medium, large).
