Membership

Why join the Chamber?

Chamber of Commerce provides opportunities to reach new customers, to increase networking, to develop your business and professional development. Also, only members can be invited to participate of the committees and community improvement.

How to became a member?

Fill the form below with your information,step 1-3, choose your membership level and click on Sign up. Do you still have questions our would prefer talk directly with a committee member, please contact our office at 333-444-5555.

Choose your membership level

Membership level selection that displays the annual fees (only one option is allowed) There are four options: Non-profit ($100), Bronze ($200), Silver ($400), Gold ($1000) The different levels have different perks which you can explain on the page in a key. Things like directory listings, business trainings, social media posts, luncheon tickets, sponsorships with levels of advertising, etc.
