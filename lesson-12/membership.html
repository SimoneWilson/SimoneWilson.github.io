Membership

Why join the Chamber?

The SLC Area Chamber of Commerce provides opportunities to reach new customers, to increase networking and to develop your business and professional skills. Also, only members can be invited to participate on the committees, contributing to community improvement.

How to become a member?

Fill out the form below with your information, steps 1-3, allow you to choose your membership level. You will receive a e-mail confirmation so, please check your e-mail inbox. We will analyze your information and within 72 business hours, you will receive a payment form link in the email you registered. If you still have questions, or would you prefer to talk directly with a committee member, please contact our office at 333-444-5555 for assistance. Our business hours are, Monday thru Friday, 8AM-5PM.

Choose your membership level

Non-profit - $100
Member directory listing, access to member directory and access to our LinkedIn Community Group.
Bronze - $200
Member directory listing, access to member directory, access to our LinkedIn Community Group, plus social media announcement and free access to animated workshops.
Silver - $400
Member directory listing, access to member directory, access to our LinkedIn Community Group, social media announcements, free access to animated workshops and free access to the events of our Chamber Board.
Gold - $1000
Member directory listing, access to member directory, access to our LinkedIn Community Group, social media announcements, free access to animated workshops, free access to the events of our Chamber Board, and the ability to list your business events on our calendar with full access to benefits, discounts and coupons.
