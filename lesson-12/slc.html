The SLC Area Chamber of Commerce.

Become a Member. Join Today.

The SLC Area Chamber of Commerce was founded in 1887 and its history is directly linked with the economic and social development of Salt Lake County. The large and traditional work offered through the committees have improved our business community. The Chamber is a non-profit business association focused on community prosperity, offering support and education for all businesses. This includes support for small business and women owners. Its leadership covers board committees and other various committees. Most important of all, the Chamber provides numerous endorsements for chamber members. Take advantage of these opportunities offered by The SLC Area Chamber of Commerce.
