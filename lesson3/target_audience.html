Audience

The website is a free resource for people and it has been created to offer a public service for the Utah's residents.

Scenarios

  • Family searching for the best days to go camping.
  • Residents getting ready to go to work.
  • Workers planning their job for the week.
  • Travelers looking for the correct outfit for the trip.
  • People getting information about weather alerts.

Personas

John Doe is a resident of Murray-UT and he loves to build new things. He is a construction worker and he is planning to replace a roof next week. He needs to know the weather forecast because it will be easier to work on the roof if it's sunny.

Mary Doe is a resident of Salt Lake City. She lives and works Downtown. She is an accountant for a Bank and she walks to work every morning. She loves to dress up and she hates feeling cold, so it's important for her to check the weather forecast every day before choosing her outfit.
