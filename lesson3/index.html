Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide accurate weather information to the public in the metropolitan area of Utah, especially to Salt Lake City residents, Murray and South Jordan.

The domain name is weathertodayutah.com. It's easy to remember and intuitive. Also, it's available on the HostGator Portal for $ 12.95 per 1year.
